What does it mean to V Active?

COVID has changed not only how we work, but also how we workout.

The whole fitness industry faced an unprecedented challenge to not only keep members engaged with the brand but also with their own personal fitness.

I believe fitness is a journey of experiences, not a single destination. The public perception of 'keeping fit' being tied to a physical gym needs to shift, to an experience that members can use anywhere and everywhere.

This is a profound opportunity for Virgin Active to lead the fitness industry creating experiences that future proof it and put it ahead of the competition.

Start the day right

Today is a new day. Whether a member has planned a workout ahead or is simply winging it, a great workout is just a touch away.

Cardio Training

There's always a million excuses not to go for a run, swim or get outside... so lets make it one less by allowing members to record every little step on their health journey.

yet every bit counts, so why not record it and build a healthy habit?

Challenges

  • Using different apps for different workouts such as strength training vs cardio, means no one source of truth!

Anytime, Anyhow, Anywhere

It doesn't matter whether you are at one of Virgin's Shiney Healthclubs on a treadmill, doing a class @ Virgin, climbing the rock wall or out and about, every bit of cardio counts.

Every bit counts, why not measure it?

Review existing workout stats such as heart rate, calories burnt, steps or repetitions. Access a past cardio workout from the calendar or pencil one in and get a reminder.

What's the game plan?


  • Milestone 1
    With health clubs still closed, it makes sense to prioritise Classes @ Home and Coaching @ Home functionality, as immediate functionality to add. This would involve coaches and dedicated recording of Exercises, with seperate encouragement and instructional tracks.
  • Milestone 2
    Classes @ Virgin and Coaching @ Virgin for a MVP can mirror the functionality currently provided by MyLocker to speed up development, with more advanced features such as notifications, advanced scheduling and stats tracking coming to later builds.
  • Milestone 3
    Cardio will be fairly small build implementing stats and wearable tracking. The benefit is that these features can be implemented across Strength Training, Coaching and Classes. Providing cross platform consistency.
  • Milestone 4
    Strength Training adds key functionality for members who prefer a self-guided strength training, allowing them to create their own routines and be sent them by Coaches @ Virgin. It adds music controls, rep counting and weight targets to workouts.

Whoever you are

My name is Leigh Masson, I'm a Sydney-based product designer, with over 6 years experience. My work includes Optus Sport, Stan, Domain, Sumo Salad and Mailplus (Australia Post). I'm a fitness enthusiast, who fell in love with the Virgin Active brand from the moment I stepped through the door.

My desire to create a fitness app stemmed from my desire to log on my resistance based workouts and take a measured approach to progression in my own fitness journey.

At the time, there was an abundance of cardio orientated apps, but not much in the strength training space. With the advent of Apple watch, I felt it was the opportune time to create a low friction app - that would act as both a resource and log without the need for 'Bro science' and loose-leaf paper.

As time went on, in talking to Coaches at Virgin - I realised that there were challenges on their end to keep clients engaged. I envisioned an app that would bridge the client-trainer gap: giving trainers a way to monitor their client's progress, assign programs and remove the hassle of juggling bookings.

No two people have the same approach to their fitness journeys. So I broadened the scope of the app to include Coaching, Classes, Cardio and Strength based workouts while also considering the implications of COVID on the fitness space and how remote classes and coaching could be facilitated.
